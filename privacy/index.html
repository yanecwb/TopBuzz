Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

To operate and improve DarkGrid Games, we may receive limited technical and usage information when you browse the site—such as device and browser type, screen size, coarse location, pages or games opened, time on page, and basic performance or error logs.

  • Device & Environment: browser/OS version, screen parameters, non‑persistent identifiers
  • Activity: navigation path, game launches, clicks/taps, timestamps
  • Diagnostics: load latency, rendering time, crash/exception records
  • Network metadata (e.g., IP) for security and analytics

How We Use Information

We use this information to keep the service running, prevent abuse, evaluate what works, and improve recommendations. We do not sell personal data.

  • Provide, monitor, and maintain core features
  • Optimize speed, reliability, and discovery quality
  • Detect misuse and enforce fair play
  • Meet safety, legal, and compliance obligations

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies or comparable storage to remember preferences (e.g., language) and measure performance. You can manage or block cookies in your browser; some features may degrade if disabled.

Partner Content

Some games are provided by partner studios and may include their own notices or controls. Please review any messages shown within those titles.

Children’s Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe data was submitted by a child, contact us to remove it promptly.

Security & Retention

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. Information is retained only as long as needed for the purposes above, then deleted or de‑identified.

Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of certain data. Submit requests via our contact page and we’ll respond in a reasonable time.
